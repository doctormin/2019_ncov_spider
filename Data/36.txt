February 18: Situation report on novel coronavirus pneumonia cases in Jiangxi province
Issued by Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 19 February 2020
　　Between 00:00 and 24:00 on February 16, Jiangxi province reported 1 new confirmed case and 52 new cured and discharged cases.
　　This one new confirmed case was reported in Nanchang. Of new cured and discharged cases, 19 were in Nanchang, 12 in Xinyu, 8 in Shangrao, 4 in Ganzhou, 3 in Jiujiang, 3 in Yichun, 1 in Piangxiang, 1 in Ji’an and 1 in Fuzhou.
　　As of 24:00 on February 18, a total of 934 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 362 cured and discharged cases and 1 fatal case. There still remained 571 confirmed cases (including 26 severe cases) and 6 suspected cases. 
　　The confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (230 cases), Xinyu (129 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases),  Pingxiang (33 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Jingdezhen (6 cases) and Ganjiang New District (1 case), respectively. Of the cured and discharged cases, 114 were in Nanchang, 58 in Xinyu, 47 in Shangrao, 33 in Jiujiang, 33 in Yichun, 29 in Fuzhou, 24 in Ganzhou, 11 in Ji’an , 7 in Pingxiang, 3 in Jingdezhen and 3 in Yingtan.
　　To date, 25104 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 21775 have been released from medical observation while 3329 are still under observation.
　　Of the 6 suspected cases, 3 were in Fuzhou, 2 in Yingtan and 1 in Jiujiang.
