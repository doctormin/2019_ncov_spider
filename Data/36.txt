February 20: Situation report on novel coronavirus pneumonia cases in Jiangxi province
Issued by Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 21 February 2020
　　Between 00:00 and 24:00 on February 20, no new confirmed case was reported in Jiangxi province, and 52 new cured cased were discharged from hospital including 12 in Nanchang, 12 in Xinyu, 11 in Yichun, 9 in Shangrao, 6 in Ganzhou, 3 in Jiujiang, 2 in Pingxiang and 2 in Fuzhou.
　　As of 24:00 on February 20, a total of 934 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 489 cured and discharged cases and 1 fatal case. There still remained 444 confirmed cases (including 26 severe cases) and 2 suspected cases. 
　　The confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (229 cases), Xinyu (130 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases),  Pingxiang (33 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Jingdezhen (6 cases) and Ganjiang New District (1 case), respectively. Of the cured and discharged cases, 145 were in Nanchang, 80 in Xinyu, 64 in Shangrao, 53 in Yichun, 47 in Jiujiang, 37 in Fuzhou, 34 in Ganzhou, 11 in Pingxiang, 11 in Ji’an, 4 in Yingtan and 3 in Jingdezhen. Of the 2 suspected cases, 1 was in Jiujiang and 1 in Fuzhou.
　　To date, 25552 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 22611 have been released from medical observation while 2941 are still under observation.
