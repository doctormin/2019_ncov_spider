Briefing on COVID-19 in Jiangxi Province on 26 February 2020
Issued by the Health Commission of Jiangxi Province on 27 February 2020
　　Between 00:00 hrs and 24:00 hrs on 26 February, no new confirmed case was reported in Jiangxi province, and 35 new cases were cured and discharged from hospital including 7 in Nanchang, 9 in Jiujiang, 2 in Pingxiang, 4 in Xinyu, 1 in Yingtan, 4 in Ganzhou, 5 in Yichun and 3 in Fuzhou.
　　As of 24:00 on 26 February, a total of 934 confirmed cases have been reported in Jiangxi province, including 754 cured and discharged cases and 1 fatality. Up till now, there are still 179 confirmed and hospitalized cases including 8 severe cases with no suspected case.
　　Of the 179 confirmed and hospitalized cases, 36 were in Nanchang, 38 in Jiujiang, 3 in Jingdezhen, 13 in Pingxiang, 15 in Xinyu, 7 in Yingtan, 14 in Ganzhou, 11 in Yichun, 22 in Shangrao, 9 in Ji’an and 11 in Fuzhou.
　　The total 934 confirmed cases were distributed in Nanchang (229 cases), Jiujiang (118 cases), Jingdezhen (6 cases), Pingxiang (33 cases), Xinyu (130 cases), Yingtan (18 cases), Ganzhou (76 cases), Yichun (106 cases), Shangrao (123 cases), Ji’an (22 cases), Fuzhou (72 cases) and Ganjiang New District(1 case), respectively.
　　Of the 754 cured and discharged cases, 193 were in Nanchang (with 1 case reduced after verification), 80 in Jiujiang, 3 in Jingdezhen, 20 in Pingxiang, 115 in Xinyu, 11 in Yingtan, 61 in Ganzhou, 95 in Yichun, 101 in Shangrao, 13 in Ji’an, 61 in Fuzhou and 1 in Ganjiang New District(with 1 case increased after verification).
　　To date, 26284 close contacts have been traced. Among them, 25050 have been released from medical observation while 1234 are still under medical observation.
